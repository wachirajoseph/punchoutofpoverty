Our Purpose

Empowering Youth Beyond Poverty

Punch Out of Poverty Organization (POPO) is committed to transforming lives in Kenya. We believe that with the right mentorship and resources, every child can break the cycle of poverty.

Our programs are community-driven, ensuring sustainable change in the heart of local neighborhoods like Ng'ando, Nairobi.

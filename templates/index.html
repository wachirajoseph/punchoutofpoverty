About POPO

POPO (Punch Out of Poverty Organization) is dedicated to transforming the lives of Kenyan youth and communities. We provide mentorship, skills training, education support, and community projects that empower individuals to break the cycle of poverty.

With deep roots in local communities, POPO ensures that our initiatives are culturally relevant, inclusive, and sustainable, creating real impact for a brighter Kenya.

Our Programs

Mentorship

Guiding Kenyan youth through personal, academic, and professional growth programs.

Skills Training

Hands-on workshops equipping youth with employable and entrepreneurial skills.

Education

Supporting formal schooling, scholarships, and informal learning to empower youth in Kenya.

Community

Strengthening communities through inclusive projects that foster resilience and solidarity.
